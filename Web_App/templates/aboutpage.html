In this project we are looking into the Global Education trends and current place both in the form of government spending and education levels. We are looking to gain insights into questions like how do educational levels affect unemployment, and does government spending affect educational levels. Through this process we will be able to get a good understanding of where global education has been, where it is now, and where it is likely heading in the future.
