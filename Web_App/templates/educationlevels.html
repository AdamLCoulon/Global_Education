This graphic shows a time series of the unemployment rates in multiple countries over time. The dataset, once cleaned, included data from over 150 countries. This visualization showcases and highlights five countries with varying unemployment rates over time. While Afghanistan’s unemployment rate remained pretty stagnant over time, Argentina’s dropped and Portugal’s increased. This can be compared with the education statistics to see if Argentina’s government spent more money on education (primary, secondary, and tertiary) in this time period. This could explain the drop in unemployment from 2000 to 2020.
