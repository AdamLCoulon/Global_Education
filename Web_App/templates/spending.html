In looking at the Global Educational spending over the last five years we see that it has been on an upward trend. By the year 2019 we see educational spending get to almost 8 billion dollars. This is almost a 40% increase from the year 2015.

In looking at the Linear Regression between % of people with a tertiary degree and total goverment spending per student we see a slight positive correlation. We have a coefficient of determination equaling 0.19, an intercept of 2148, and a slope of 387. Again, there is a slightly positive correlation telling us that as global education spending continues to increase we can expect to see % of tertiary education to increase as well.
